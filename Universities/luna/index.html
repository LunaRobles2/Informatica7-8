Universidad Pedagógica Nacional

Key Academic Offerings:

Licenciaturas (Undergraduate): Programs include Pedagogy, Educational Intervention, Primary Education for Indigenous Environments, Educational Psychology, and Pre-school Education. Graduate Programs: Master's degrees in Basic Education and Higher Education, as well as doctorates in educational development. Diverse Options: Beyond standard degrees, UPN offers specializations and diplomas.

Program Highlights:

The UPN Unidad Ajusco acts as the main campus responsible for the design of programs implemented nationwide. Specific units, such as UPN 20-A, offer specialized tracks like Indigenous Primary Education and Educational Intervention. The number of programs varies by specific unit, though they share a unified core curriculum.
