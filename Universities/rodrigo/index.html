Universidad Autónoma de Chihuahua

About it:

The Universidad Autónoma de Chihuahua (UACH) is a public institution of higher education based in Chihuahua, Mexico, founded in 1954. As the maximum house of studies in the state, it serves over 25,000 students across 15 faculties with a focus on sustainable, social, and economic development.

Sports: The UACH sports teams are known as the Águilas UACH (American football), which have won several national conferences (2004, 2008, 2022, 2023), and UACH F.C. (soccer).

Culture: The university promotes culture and arts, including the maintenance of the Instituto Confucio and the production of cultural content.

Number of programs:

The Universidad Autónoma de Chihuahua (UACH) offers more than 50 undergraduate programs (licenciaturas e ingenierías) distributed across its 15 faculties.

Key details regarding UACH's academic offering include:

⚫Faculties: 15 distinct academic units.

⚫Study Areas: The programs cover eight main areas of knowledge, including health sciences, social sciences, and administration.

⚫Modalities: Programs are available in both face-to-face and virtual (online) formats.

⚫Academic Levels: The institution offers degrees (pregrado), master's degrees (maestrías), and doctoral programs.
